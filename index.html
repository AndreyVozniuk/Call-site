Debt Solutions
It is always best to seek professional advice about your ﬁnancial situation before your debt begins to spiral out of control. There are a number of debt solutions available in the UK.
Each debt solution is different, yet they are all designed to help you clear your debts while and guarantee that as you do so you keep enought of your income to keep the lights on and put food on the table. Some debt solutions even allow your debts to be wiped clean after a few years.
Each debt solution has its own set of advantages, disadvantages and eligibility requirements, so it is important that you understand which is most suited to your circumstances.
Some of the debt solutions that may be available to you are:
Council Tax Repayment Plans
Individual Voluntary Arrangements (IVA)
